# IP in menu bar Changelog

## [Initial Version] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- Shows your **public or local IP** (IPv4 or IPv6) directly in the menu bar
- Click any address to **copy it to the clipboard**
- Supports **public IPv4, public IPv6, local IPv4, and local IPv6**
- Supports multiple **local network adapters** — pick which one to display in the menu bar
- Automatically **refreshes public IP** every 15 minutes or when your network changes
